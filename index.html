Recurring Deposit (RD) Formula:

Maturity Value = P × [((1 + r)ⁿ - 1) / r] × (1 + r)

Where: P = Monthly deposit amount, r = Monthly interest rate (Annual rate / 12 / 100), n = Total number of months (Years × 12)

Example: For ₹5,000/month for 10 years at 6.5% p.a., the calculation uses monthly compounding to arrive at the maturity value.

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) Formula:

Future Value = P × [((1 + r)ⁿ - 1) / r] × (1 + r)

Where: P = Monthly investment amount, r = Monthly expected return rate (Annual return / 12 / 100), n = Total number of months (Years × 12)

Example: For ₹5,000/month for 10 years at 12% p.a. assumed return, the calculation uses monthly compounding. Note: SIP returns are market-linked and not guaranteed.
